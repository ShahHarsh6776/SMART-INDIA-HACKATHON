About Us

CIVICSPLAY

Mission: To educate and empower citizens, especially young people, by making civics and constitutional literacy engaging and relatable.

Methods: Utilizes creative approaches such as storytelling, theater, role-plays, and games to make complex concepts more understandable and enjoyable.

Target Audience: Primarily aims at students, educators, and general citizens interested in learning about their rights, duties, and the functioning of Indian democracy.

Impact: Aims to create a more informed and active citizenry that can engage effectively in the democratic process and uphold the principles of the Constitution of India.

Members: Harsh shah, Ayushi Kotadiya, Bhagyesh patil, Ayush patel, Palak sankharva, Dhani patel.
